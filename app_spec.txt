<project_specification>
  <project_name>TG-Ticket-Agent</project_name>

  <overview>
    Multi-agent Telegram bot for selling event tickets via Bill24/TixGear platform. Users access the bot through agent-specific deep links, browse events, select seats via WebApp (modified Bill24 widget), pay through integrated acquiring, and receive tickets with QR codes directly in Telegram. The system replaces email-based authentication with Telegram Chat-ID, supports multiple ticket agents, and includes an admin panel for management.
  </overview>
